# HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

**Interactive Power BI dashboard** analyzing employee attrition using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Dataset (1,470 employees).

### Key Insights Delivered
- Overall attrition rate: **16.12%** (significantly above industry benchmark)
- Highest-risk roles: Sales Representatives (39.7%), Laboratory Technicians (23.9%), HR (23.0%)
- Primary drivers: Low salary, overtime, short tenure (<3 years), and low job satisfaction
- Retention sweet spot: Employees with 6+ years tenure show >80% lower attrition
- Gender insight: Female attrition rate (17.0%) higher than male (14.8%) despite smaller headcount

### Dashboard Pages
1. **Attrition Overview & Risk Drivers** – KPI, risk tree, key influencers, actionable recommendations
2. **Key Drivers & Retention Forecast** – Income vs tenure, job satisfaction heat-map, gender rate, tenure-based attrition forecast
3. **Detailed Attrition Breakdown** – By income band, department, job role, age, education

### Features
- Fully interactive slicers (Department, Job Role, Overtime, Gender, Age)
- Conditional formatting & clear color coding (orange = high risk, blue = low risk)
- Clean dark theme with readable labels

**Data Source**: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset (public dataset)  
**Tool**: Microsoft Power BI Desktop
